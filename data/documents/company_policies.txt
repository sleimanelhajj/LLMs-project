WAREHOUSE SOLUTIONS INC.
COMPANY POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
Last Updated: January 2024

================================================================================
1. RETURNS AND REFUNDS POLICY
================================================================================

1.1 Return Window
- Products may be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Items must be in original condition and packaging
- Return authorization number required (call customer service)

1.2 Refund Process
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item is defective

1.3 Non-Returnable Items
- Custom-cut ropes or wires
- Items marked as "final sale"
- Products showing signs of use or damage

1.4 Defective Items
- Report defects within 48 hours of delivery
- Photo documentation required
- Replacement or full refund provided

================================================================================
2. SHIPPING AND DELIVERY POLICY
================================================================================

2.1 Processing Time
- Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship same day
- Custom orders: 3-5 business days processing
- Bulk orders: 5-7 business days processing

2.2 Shipping Methods
- Standard: 5 business days
- Express: 2 business days
- Overnight: Next business day (orders before 12 PM)
- International: 7-14 business days

2.3 Delivery Issues
- Contact us within 3 days of expected delivery
- Re-ship or refund for lost packages
- Signature required for orders over $500

================================================================================
3. PRICING AND PAYMENT POLICY
================================================================================

3.1 Price Matching
- We match competitor prices on identical products
- Valid within 7 days of purchase
- Requires proof of competitor pricing

3.2 Bulk Discounts
- 10% discount on orders over $1,000
- 15% discount on orders over $5,000
- 20% discount on orders over $10,000
- Custom quotes available for larger orders

3.3 Payment Terms
- Net 30 terms available for approved business accounts
- Credit card, ACH, wire transfer accepted
- Purchase orders accepted from established customers

3.4 Sales Tax
- Collected based on delivery address
- Tax-exempt organizations must provide valid certificate
- Resale certificates accepted for wholesale purchases

================================================================================
4. QUALITY ASSURANCE POLICY
================================================================================

4.1 Product Standards
- All products meet OSHA safety standards
- ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing
- Regular quality inspections performed

4.2 Certifications
- Material certifications available upon request
- Test reports provided for bulk orders
- Certificate of conformance included

4.3 Warranty
- 1-year warranty on all products
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects only
- Normal wear and tear not covered

================================================================================
5. CUSTOMER ACCOUNT POLICY
================================================================================

5.1 Account Creation
- Business accounts available for companies
- Credit application required for Net 30 terms
- Approval typically within 24-48 hours

5.2 Account Benefits
- Volume pricing automatically applied
- Order history and tracking
- Dedicated account manager for large accounts
- Priority customer service

5.3 Account Security
- Password must be changed every 90 days
- Two-factor authentication available
- Notify us immediately of unauthorized access

================================================================================
6. ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY
================================================================================

6.1 Sustainability
- All packaging materials are recyclable
- We use recycled materials when possible
- Carbon-neutral shipping options available

6.2 Product Recycling
- Take-back program for worn products
- Proper disposal instructions provided
- Recycling partners in major cities

================================================================================
7. PRIVACY AND DATA POLICY
================================================================================

7.1 Data Collection
- We collect only necessary business information
- Credit card data encrypted and not stored
- Third-party payment processors used

7.2 Data Usage
- Information used for order processing only
- Marketing emails opt-in required
- Data never sold to third parties

7.3 Data Retention
- Order history retained for 7 years
- Account data deleted upon request
- Compliance with GDPR and CCPA

================================================================================
8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION POLICY
================================================================================

8.1 Complaint Process
- Contact customer service first
- Escalation to management if unresolved
- Written response within 48 hours

8.2 Mediation
- Third-party mediation available
- Costs shared equally
- Binding arbitration if mediation fails

8.3 Legal Action
- Jurisdiction: Chicago, Illinois
- Governed by Illinois state law
- Small claims court for disputes under $10,000
